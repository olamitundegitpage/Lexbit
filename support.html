Frequently Asked Questions

Account Questions

To create an account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the top right of any page
  2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
  3. Verify your email through the confirmation link we send
  4. Complete your profile with required information
  5. Set up two-factor authentication for added security

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new strong password
  5. Log in with your new credentials

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Deposits & Withdrawals

Crypto deposits typically require blockchain confirmations:

  • Bitcoin (BTC): 3 confirmations (~30 minutes)
  • Ethereum (ETH): 12 confirmations (~5 minutes)
  • USDT/USDC: 12 confirmations (~5 minutes)
  • Other coins: Varies by network

Fiat deposits via bank transfer take 1-3 business days.

Withdrawals may be pending for several reasons:

  • Security hold: Large withdrawals require manual review (up to 24 hours)
  • Incomplete verification: Verify your identity if prompted
  • Network congestion: Blockchain networks sometimes experience delays
  • Scheduled maintenance: Check our status page for system updates

If your withdrawal remains pending beyond expected timeframes, please contact support.

Security

To enable 2FA for enhanced security:

  1. Log in to your LexBit account
  2. Go to "Security Settings" in your account dashboard
  3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  4. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  5. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to verify
  6. Download and store your backup codes in a secure location

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA to protect your account.

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account:

  1. Immediately change your password
  2. Revoke any active sessions in "Security Settings"
  3. Check your withdrawal history for unauthorized transactions
  4. Contact LexBit Support immediately
  5. Enable 2FA if you haven't already

Our security team will investigate and help secure your account.
